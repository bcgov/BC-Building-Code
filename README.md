# BC Building Code
The BC Building Code 2024 (BCBC) establishes the provincial standards for construction, renovations, repairs, and demolitions in British Columbia. It ensures buildings meet high standards for safety, health, accessibility, fire protection, structural stability, and environmental sustainability, including energy and water efficiency. The BCBC 2024 is based on the National Building Codes of Canada, with adjustments for the unique needs of BC, and applies across British Columbia, excluding federal lands and the City of Vancouver.

The BCBC 2024 is built on the foundation of the National Codes of Canada, with custom adjustments to address the unique needs of British Columbia. It remains a leading standard for excellence in construction and sustainability. The BCBC 2024 continues to set the benchmark for building excellence and sustainability.

This repository provides the BC Building Code in JSON format, enabling developers, local Jurisdictions, and partners to integrate and interact with structured building code efficiently.
